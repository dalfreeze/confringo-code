HOGWARTS CLAIMS

This will be where you can claim your character's Hogwarts Quidditch positions and whether or not they were a Head Student all based on their 7th (or final) year at Hogwarts starting September of the start of term to May of the next year when they graduated. Because there were so many prefects, we don't need you to worry about claiming those. If you need help figuring out when your character was in their 7th year, you can reach out to staff for assistance or use the age/date calculator in our guidebook. We're not really looking to be strict here. This is more of a way to find links to other characters with whom you may not have plotted before.

Regarding Head Student positions: no two students of the same gender. For example, if one spot in a particular year is claimed by a man, the remaining position must go to a woman or non-binary character. Please remove the asterisk when posting your code.
